Cilantro Lime Dressing

This Cilantro Lime Dressing is your new go-to. It's vibrant. It's zesty. It sneakily packs in probiotics from the addition of greek yogurt. What more could you want? This dressing levels up salad, tacos and grilled veggies. Perfectly balanced with a touch of honey or maple syrup, it's as versatile as it is delicious!

The Good Stuff

2 cups of cilantro

1 garlic clove

1/4 cup lime juice

2 tsp honey

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 tsp ground coriander

1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

1/2 cup of greek yogurt
